Dubai VIP (Private)

Dubai, Dubai | 6 Days

Day 1 : Dubai

Arrival at Dubai Airport, reception, and private transfer to the hotel for accommodation.

Day 2 : Dubai Classic City Tour with Burj Khalifa Entrance & Dhow Cruise Marina

Breakfast, then a private classic Dubai city tour. Enjoy magnificent views of Dubai Creek, passing through the Bastakiya Heritage Area with its fascinating old houses and traditional wind towers built by wealthy merchants.

Visit the 225-year-old Al Fahidi Fort, then board a traditional Abra boat to cross the creek and explore the Spice Market and the Gold Souk.

Continue along Jumeirah Road with views of Jumeirah Mosque and a photo stop at Burj Al Arab, the world’s only 7-star hotel.

Entrance to Burj Khalifa, the tallest building in the world. Return to the hotel on your own.

In the evening, depart to enjoy the sights and sounds of Dubai Marina aboard a traditional Dhow cruise for two hours. Buffet dinner included. Return to the hotel for accommodation.

Day 3 : Abu Dhabi with Lunch at Emirates Palace & Ferrari World

Breakfast. Private overland drive (approx. 2 hours) from Dubai, passing Jebel Ali Port, the world’s largest man-made port, to the capital of the UAE.

Photo stop at Al Jazira Residence, the former residence of Sheikh Zayed. Visit the Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque, the third largest mosque in the world, and the mausoleum of Sheikh Zayed, the late President of the UAE and Father of the Nation.

Continue to Al Maqta Bridge, passing through one of Abu Dhabi’s wealthiest areas, the Ministers’ Area.

Arrive at the Corniche, often compared to Manhattan. Lunch at Emirates Palace Hotel, one of the most luxurious hotels ever built, featuring its own helipad and marina.

Continue to Al Bateen Area, home to the Royal Family palaces.

Proceed to Ferrari World and enter the theme park (Bronze Ticket included).

Return to Dubai for accommodation.

Day 4 : Dubai

Breakfast at the hotel. Free day at leisure.

Day 5 : Lost Chambers Aquarium Visit & Dinner at Atlantis The Palm

Breakfast at the hotel. Pick-up at the scheduled time and transfer to Atlantis The Palm to visit the Lost Chambers Aquarium, followed by dinner at Kaleidoscope Restaurant. Return to the hotel for accommodation.

Day 6 : Dubai

Breakfast, then at the scheduled time private transfer to the airport for your return flight.
