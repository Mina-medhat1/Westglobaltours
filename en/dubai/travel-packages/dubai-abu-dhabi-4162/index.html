Dubai & Abu Dhabi

Dubai, Dubai | 6 Days

Day 1 : Dubai

Arrival at Dubai Airport, reception, and transfer to the hotel for accommodation.

Day 2 : Dubai Classic City Tour

Breakfast, then a classic Dubai city tour. Enjoy magnificent views of Dubai Creek, passing through the Bastakiya Heritage Area with its fascinating old houses and traditional wind towers built by wealthy merchants.

Next, visit the 225-year-old Al Fahidi Fort. Then board a traditional Abra boat to cross the creek and visit the Spice Market and the Gold Souk.

Continue along Jumeirah Road with views of the Jumeirah Mosque, followed by a photo stop at Burj Al Arab, the world’s only 7-star hotel.

Drive past Burj Khalifa, the tallest building in the world, the World Trade Center, and the International Financial Centre.

Accommodation in Dubai.

Day 3 : Dubai / Abu Dhabi

Breakfast. A 2-hour drive from Dubai, passing Jebel Ali Port, the world’s largest man-made port, to the capital of the UAE.

Photo stop at Al Jazira Residence, the former residence of Sheikh Zayed. Visit the Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque, the third largest mosque in the world, and the mausoleum of Sheikh Zayed, the late President of the UAE and Father of the Nation.

Continue to Al Maqta Bridge, passing through one of Abu Dhabi’s wealthiest areas, the Ministers’ Area.

Arrival at the Corniche, often compared to Manhattan. Photo stop at Emirates Palace Hotel, known as one of the most expensive hotels ever built, with its own helipad and marina.

Continue to Al Bateen Area, home to the Royal Family palaces.

Accommodation in Abu Dhabi.

Day 4 : Abu Dhabi

Breakfast. Free day. Accommodation in Abu Dhabi.

Day 5 : Abu Dhabi / Dubai

Breakfast. Overland transfer Abu Dhabi to Dubai by air-conditioned vehicle.

Accommodation in Dubai.

4x4 Desert Safari / BBQ Dinner

Breakfast at the hotel. Free morning. In the afternoon, enjoy our most popular excursion. Land Cruisers (6 persons per vehicle) will pick you up for an exciting drive across the high sand dunes. Capture unforgettable photos of the Arabian sunset.

After sunset, proceed to the desert camp. The aroma of freshly grilled kebabs and lamb, campfires, traditional shisha, and relaxing Arabic music create an unforgettable evening.

After a sumptuous dinner, a belly dancer will perform, and henna painting will be available. Water, soft drinks, tea, and coffee are included.

Return to the hotel for accommodation.

Day 6 : Dubai

Breakfast, then at the scheduled time transfer to the airport to take your return flight.
