Ramses II

3 Nights Nile Cruise + 1 Night Abu Simbel + 3 Nights Cairo

A magnificent tour designed to explore the glory of ancient Egypt.

This program includes an excursion to the Pyramids of Giza and the Egyptian Museum, along with the treasures of Upper Egypt — Luxor, Aswan, and the spectacular Abu Simbel Temples.

Carved into the rock, the Abu Simbel complex consists of two temples: one dedicated to Ramses II and the other to Queen Nefertari, his beloved wife. Construction of the temples took nearly 20 years and was completed during the reign of Ramses II (1279–1213 BC).
