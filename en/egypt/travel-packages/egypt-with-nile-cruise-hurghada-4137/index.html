Egypt with Nile Cruise & Hurghada

Cairo, Egypt | 11 Days

4 Nights Cairo + 3 Nights Nile Cruise + 3 Nights Hurghada

Enjoy the perfect combination of cultural discovery and seaside relaxation.

This program includes the majestic monuments of Cairo, a 3-night cruise along the Nile between Aswan and Luxor, and a relaxing stay in Hurghada on the stunning Red Sea coast.

A journey that blends ancient history, natural beauty, and unforgettable experiences.

Day 1 : Arrival in Cairo

Arrival at Cairo International Airport.

Meet & assist by our Spanish-speaking representative before passport control.

Transfer to the hotel and overnight in Cairo.

Day 2 : Cairo | Pyramids of Giza

Breakfast at the hotel.

Departure for an included visit to the Three Pyramids of Giza, the Eternal Sphinx, and the Valley Temple.

(Entrance inside the pyramids not included).

Afternoon free, with an optional visit to Saqqara and Memphis, the ancient capital of Egypt.

Optional evening activity: Sound & Light Show at the Pyramids.

Return to the hotel and overnight.

Day 3 : Cairo | Free Day or Optional Full-Day Tour

Breakfast at the hotel.

Free day at leisure or optional full-day tour of Cairo including:

The Egyptian Museum of Pharaonic Art

The Citadel of Saladin and the Alabaster Mosque

Khan El Khalili Bazaar

Old Coptic Cairo

Optional evening: Nile Dinner Cruise.

Overnight in Cairo.

Day 4 : Cairo → Aswan | Start Nile Cruise

Breakfast at the hotel.

Transfer to Cairo Airport and flight to Aswan.

Arrival and transfer to the cruise ship.

Lunch on board.

Later, visit:

Philae Temple, dedicated to the goddess Isis

Felucca ride on the Nile, passing by:

The Mausoleum of Agha Khan

Elephantine Island

The Botanical Garden

Dinner and overnight on board.

Day 5 : Aswan → Kom Ombo

Breakfast on board.

Optional excursion: Abu Simbel Temples.

In the afternoon, sail to Kom Ombo and visit the unique double temple dedicated to:

Sobek, the crocodile god

Haroeris, the falcon-headed god

Dinner and overnight on board.

Day 6 : Edfu → Esna → Luxor (Full Board)

Arrival in Edfu.

Visit the impressive Temple of Horus, one of the best-preserved temples in Egypt.

Sail toward Esna and cross the Esna Lock.

Continue sailing to Luxor.

Dinner and overnight on board.

Day 7 : Luxor → Hurghada

Breakfast on board.

Visit the East Bank and West Bank of Luxor:

Temples of Luxor and Karnak

Valley of the Kings

Temple of Queen Hatshepsut (Deir El Bahari)

Colossi of Memnon

Disembarkation and transfer by road to Hurghada, located on the Red Sea coast.

Arrival at the hotel.

Dinner and overnight in Hurghada.

Day 8 : Hurghada | Red Sea Leisure

Half board accommodation.

Free days to enjoy the Red Sea beaches, optional snorkeling and diving trips, desert safaris, boat excursions, and more.

Overnight in Hurghada.

Day 9 : Hurghada | Red Sea Leisure

Half board accommodation.

Free days to enjoy the Red Sea beaches, optional snorkeling and diving trips, desert safaris, boat excursions, and more.

Overnight in Hurghada.

Day 10 : Hurghada → Cairo

Breakfast at the hotel.

Transfer to Hurghada Airport for flight to Cairo.

Arrival and transfer to the hotel.

Optional evening: Nile Dinner Cruise.

Overnight in Cairo.

Day 11 : Cairo | Departure

Breakfast at the hotel.

Transfer to Cairo Airport for your international flight.
