Honeymoon in Egypt

Cairo, Egypt | 10 Days

3 Nights Cairo + 3 Nights Nile Cruise + 3 Nights Sharm El Sheikh

Celebrate love with an unforgettable honeymoon in Egypt — a perfect blend of ancient wonders, romantic Nile sailing, and relaxing days on the shores of the Red Sea.

Your journey begins in Cairo with the iconic Pyramids, continues with a magical cruise between Aswan and Luxor, and ends with a serene escape in Sharm El Sheikh, one of the world’s premier beach and diving destinations.

A dreamy experience designed for couples seeking culture, adventure, and pure relaxation.

Day 1 : Arrival in Cairo

Arrival at Cairo International Airport.

Meet & assist by our Spanish-speaking representative.

Transfer to the hotel and overnight stay in Cairo.

Day 2 : Cairo | Pyramids of Giza

Breakfast at the hotel.

Morning visit to the Three Pyramids of Giza, the Sphinx, and the Valley Temple.

Your tour includes:

Stop at the Pyramid of Khufu (Cheops) or Khafre (Kefrén) for panoramic views

A full view of the entire Giza Plateau

Final stop at the legendary Sphinx

Return to the hotel and overnight.

Day 3 : Cairo → Aswan | Start Nile Cruise

Breakfast, then transfer to Cairo Domestic Airport for your flight to Aswan.

Arrival, assistance, and transfer to the cruise ship.

Embarkation followed by a visit to:

Philae Temple

Set on an island surrounded by the Nile, Philae is one of the most beautiful temples from the Greco-Roman era. Access is by motorboat.

Felucca Ride

Enjoy one of the most peaceful and authentic experiences: sailing on a traditional felucca around Elephantine Island, the Botanical Garden, and the Agha Khan Mausoleum.

Overnight on board in Aswan.

Day 4 : Kom Ombo → Edfu

Full board on board.

Sail to Kom Ombo in the afternoon and visit the Temple of Sobek and Haroeris — a unique double temple dedicated to the crocodile god and the falcon god.

Continue sailing to Edfu.

Overnight in Edfu.

Day 5 : Edfu → Esna → Luxor

Full board on board.

Morning visit to the Temple of Horus in Edfu:

Accessible by horse-drawn carriage or local transfer, this temple is the second largest and one of the most complete in Egypt, showcasing a fascinating mix of Egyptian and Greek architectural styles.

Sail to Esna, cross the Esna Lock, and continue to Luxor.

Overnight on board in Luxor.

Day 6 : Luxor → Cairo

Breakfast and disembarkation.

Morning visit to the West Bank of Luxor:

Valley of the Kings – home to 63 tombs from the New Kingdom

Temple of Queen Hatshepsut (Deir el Bahari) – an architectural masterpiece

Colossi of Memnon – two massive statues of Amenhotep III

Afternoon visit to the East Bank:

Karnak Temple – the largest religious complex ever built

Luxor Temple – a stunning monument built by Tukmosis III and Ramses II

Transfer to Luxor Airport for flight to Cairo.

Arrival and transfer to the hotel.

Overnight in Cairo.

Day 7 : Cairo → Sharm El Sheikh

Breakfast, then transfer to Cairo Airport for your flight to Sharm El Sheikh.

Arrival, assistance, and transfer to your hotel.

Dinner and overnight.

Day 8 : Sharm El Sheikh | Leisure Day

Breakfast.

Free day to enjoy the Red Sea, diving, snorkeling, or optional desert adventures.

Dinner and overnight at the hotel.

Day 9 : Sharm El Sheikh | Leisure Day

Breakfast.

Another day at leisure to relax on the beach or enjoy optional excursions.

Dinner and overnight at the hotel.

Day 10 : Sharm El Sheikh → Cairo → Departure

Breakfast, then transfer to Sharm Airport for your flight to Cairo and connection with your international departure flight.
